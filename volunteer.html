Presentation of the structure of the association

The association is divided into 4 departments:

  • Administrative department
  • Communication department
  • Integration department
  • Educational department

The association is governed by the Board of Directors which has 5 members elected by vote at the General Assembly.

The communication, integration and educational department welcome volunteers in their teams:

Educational department :

  • French Corners team
  • Educational Content team

Integration department :

  • Welcome team
  • Technical Support Team
  • Moderators team

Communication department

  • Conquest team
  • Design team
  • Media team

Educational department

The educational department is divided into 3 teams.

French Corners team

  • Organize French Corners for advanced students on Google Meet
  • Organize French Corners for intermediate students on Google Meet
  • Organize French Corners for beginners in Arabic on Google Meet
  • Organize French Corners for beginners in English on Google Meet
  • Organize French Corners for beginners in Spanish on Google Meet

Educational Content team

  • Prepare courses on Google Docs
  • Prepare educational content for social media on Airtable

Communication department

The communication department is divided into 3 teams.

Design team

  • Prepare content for social media on Canva
  • Post content on social media on Publer

Conquest team

  • Contact new students on social media
  • Contact new volunteers on social media

Media team

  • Write articles for the SupEducation blog on Google Docs
  • Review written articles for the SupEducation blog on Google Docs

Integration department

The integration department is divided into 2 teams.

Welcome team

  • Organize welcome sessions for new members in English on Workplace and Google Meet
  • Organize welcome sessions for new members in French on Workplace and Google Meet
  • Organize welcome sessions for new members in Arabic on Workplace and Google Meet

Technical support team

  • Help new members joining the community in English on Telegram
  • Help new members joining the community in French on Telegram
  • Help new members joining the community in Arabic on Telegram

Moderators team

  • Moderate the advanced students community in Workplace
  • Moderate the intermediate students community in Workplace
  • Moderate the beginner students community in Arabic in Workplace
  • Moderate the beginner students community in English in Workplace
  • Moderate the beginner students community in Spanish in Workplace
