Sign up for free once only (Do not sign up multiple times)

Your phone number, personal email and date of birth will not be shared to anyone. We need this information to legally identify each member of our community.

You will be automatically assigned a new email address, firstname.lastname@supeducation.org with a password that you may change upon your first log in.

Make sure that you type your real first and last name correctly in a latin alphabet without typo. You will not be able to change it later.

Make sure that your email address is correct or otherwise you will not be able to receive your SupEducation email and password to join our community

Sign up for free

Receive your SupEducation email and password

When you sign up, a SupEducation email will be automatically created. It is a Google Workspace account. You can log in on Gmail and Google Calendar the same way as any Google account. You will receive this new email and password on the email provided upon registration.

If you do not receive this email, check your spam folder and DO NOT sign up again. If you encounters technical issues, please contact us on Telegram.

Join our community on Workplace

"Workplace from Facebook" is our main communication app. You can access it directly on https://supeducation.workplace.com/

On a mobile phone, the app is very convenient to use although the first login can be difficult sometimes. Contact us on Telegram if you need assistance.

  • Download for Android
  • Download for iOS

Workplace also use another app : "Workplace Chat". Do not download it, students don't use it in SupEducation. It's only for volunteers only.

Contact us on Telegram

You will receive your login and password by email after your registration. If you don't receive this email within 48 hours or if you need assistance you can contact us on Telegram.

  • Support in English
  • Support in French
  • Support in Arabic

There are no paid employees. We are all volunteers who are not available 24/7 even though we do our best to get back to our members as soon as possible.
